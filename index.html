Moje ime je Dunja Mamula i ovo je moja stranica.

People that love rain are called pluviophiles.

The word pluviophile comes from the Latin word “pluvial,” which means rain, and “phile,” which denotes a thing or a person.

Therefore, a pluviophile is a lover of rain, or someone that finds joy and peace of mind during the rainy days.
